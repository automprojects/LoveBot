Steps to perform the task:
1. Download main.py and open this file with your favourite IDE.
2. Before running this code, check if you have installed pyautogui. Otherwise type 'pip install puautogui' in terminal and hit enter.
3. Next open web.whatsapp.com, login to your whatsapp account and choose the target chatbox of your lover.
4. Verify if the text cursor is blinking inside the "Type a message" Box.
5. Now run the code, it prints "Program starts in 5 seconds....." in the terminal. Then switch to whatsapp page and wait for it to perform task.
6. It starts typing and sending the text multiple types until loop ends. 
7. To stop this execution click inside terminal and click Ctrl + C.
8. Have fun. Bye
